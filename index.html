Welcome to LanguagesAnywhere!!


Ever wanted to learn the basics of Chinese, Hindi, or Spanish? Well, then Languages Anywhere is the resource for you! Mission Statement

Provide free and accessible language education resources for anyone with internet access.

How? Starting with the simple idea of creating a website, we expanded that idea into a website to tutor people in languages, later adding the idea of quizzes. Eventually we decided to create this website to help make learning languages online a seamless, completely free experience for people by organizing a curriculum into a digestable page of content.

Chinese

Chinese is one of only two world languages to be spoken by over a billion people. With over 900 million native speakers, Chinese is one of the most used mother tongues. It is also one of the oldest languages still in use, with basics dating back as far as the Shang Dynasty (1600-1046 BCE). Being such a widely spoken and well known language makes Chinese an attractive option for many Westerners when choosing a new language to learn.

Hindi

Hindi is spoken by more than 350 million people globally, Hindi is the third most spoken language in the world, behind only Chinese and English. Hindi is the official language of India. Hindi is widely spoken in India and around the world by many people.

Spanish

Spanish is one of the most commonly spoken languages as the fourth most spoken language globally, with nearly 550 million speakers. Originating in Spain, Spanish is a Romance language with influence from Latin, Arabic, and French. Spanish being the official language in 21 countries, has become popular in Spain, Central, and South America. For anyone traveling to those countries, knowing some basic Spanish would be a good choice.
