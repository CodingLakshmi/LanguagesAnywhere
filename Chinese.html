Basic Greetings and Common Phrases

Basic Greetings


Hello - 你好

/nǐ hǎo/


Goodbye - 再见

/zài jiàn/


How are you? - 你好吗？

/nǐ hǎo ma/


Good Morning - 早上好

/zǎo shang hǎo/


Good Afternoon - 下午好

/xià wǔ hǎo/


Goodnight - 晚上好

/wǎn shàng hǎo/

Common Phrases

Friday - 星期五

Sunday - 星期天

/xīng qī tiān/


Example Conversations


Q: Hi! How are you? 你好吗？

/nǐ hǎo ma?/

A: I’m great, thank you! 我很好，谢谢！

/wǒ hěn hǎo, xiè xie/


Q: What day of the week is it today? 今天是星期几？

/jīn tiān shì xīng qī jǐ?/

A: Today is Sunday 今天是星期天。

/jīn tiān shì xīng qī tiān/


Asking for Directions

Basic Sentences


Where is ___? - ___在哪里？

/zài nǎ lǐ/


How far is ___? - ___多远?

/duō yuǎn/


我怎么去那里？

/wǒ zěn me qù nà li/


Phrases for Navigating


Direction - 方向

/fāng xiàng/


Place - 地方

/dì fāng/


Left - 左边

/zuǒ bian/


To the left of (the) ___ - 在___的左边

/zài___de zuǒ bian/


Right - 右边

/yòu bian/


To the right of (the) ___ - 在___的右边

/zài___de yòu bian/


Straight ahead - 前方

/qián fāng/


Near - 进

/jìn/


Far - 远

/yuǎn/


Destinations


Store - 商店

/shāng diàn/


Supermarket - 超级市场

/chāo jí shì chǎng/


Restaurant - 餐厅

/cān tīng/


Park - 公园

/gōng yuán/


Bank - 银行

/yín háng/


Hospital - 医院

/yī yuàn/


Example Conversations


Q: Where is the park? 公园在哪里？

/gōng yuán zài nǎ lǐ?/

A: The park is near the supermarket. 公园离超级市场很近。

/gōng yuán lí chāo jí shì chǎng hěn jìn/


Q: How far is the hospital from here? 医院离这里多远？

/yī yuàn lí zhè lǐ duō yuǎn?/

A: The hospital is very far from here. 医院离这里很远。

/yī yuàn lí zhè lǐ hěn yuǎn./


Q: How do I get to the restaurant? 从这里要怎么到餐厅？

/cóng zhè lǐ yào zěn me dào cān tīng？/

A: Go ahead, make a right, and you will see the restaurant. 直走，再右转，你就会看到餐厅。

/zhí zǒu，zài yòu zhuǎn，nǐ jiù huì kàn dào cān tīng/

Example Conversations


Q: What’s the weather like today? 今天天气怎么样？

/jīn tiān tiān qì zěn me yàng?/

A: It is sunny and hot. 今天天气晴朗炎热

/jīn tiān tiān qì qíng lǎng yán rè/


Q: Will it rain tomorrow? 明天会下雨吗？

/Míng tiān huì xià yǔ ma?/

A: The weather report says it will rain tomorrow. 气象预报说明天会下雨。

/qì xiàng yù bào shuō míng tiān huì xià yǔ/
